About Newton Travel Agency

Connecting You to the Heart of East Africa Since 1999

Our Story

Founded in 1999, Newton Travel Agency was born from a dream to open the world to the hidden wonders of East Africa. What began as a small family-owned tour company in Nairobi has grown into a full-service travel agency that connects travelers to Kenya, Tanzania, Burundi, Uganda, and beyond. For over two decades, we’ve guided adventurers through breathtaking savannahs, serene coastlines, and vibrant cities — curating experiences that reveal the soul of Africa.

Our philosophy is simple: travel should not just be about seeing places, but about feeling them. We believe in creating sustainable tourism that benefits both the traveler and the local communities. Our partnerships with local guides, conservation projects, and cultural organizations ensure that every trip leaves a positive footprint.

Our Mission

To make travel an inspiring, life-changing experience for every explorer. We specialize in custom safaris, city tours, beach holidays, and cultural immersions designed to reflect your personality and passion for discovery.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s most trusted and innovative travel company, blending adventure with sustainability. We aim to preserve natural heritage, celebrate local culture, and create connections that last long after the journey ends.

Why Choose Us

Newton Travel Agency combines over 25 years of expertise with heartfelt service. Our experienced team of travel designers, guides, and coordinators ensures every detail — from airport pickup to your last sunset — is perfectly handled. We provide tailored itineraries, multilingual guides, 24/7 customer support, and insider access to the region’s most exclusive spots.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team includes local experts, adventure lovers, and passionate storytellers who live and breathe travel. Together, we ensure every journey is not just memorable but transformative. Whether you’re seeking a safari through the Maasai Mara, a cultural visit in Stone Town, or a peaceful retreat on Lake Tanganyika, we’re here to make it happen with warmth, professionalism, and authenticity.
