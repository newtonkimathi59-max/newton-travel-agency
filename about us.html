Our Story

Founded in 1999, Newton Travel Agency was born from a dream to open the world to the hidden wonders of East Africa. What began as a small family-owned tour company in Nairobi has grown into a full-service travel agency that connects travelers to Kenya, Tanzania, Burundi, Uganda, and beyond.

Our philosophy is simple: travel should not just be about seeing places, but about feeling them. We believe in creating sustainable tourism that benefits both the traveler and the local communities.

Our Mission

To make travel an inspiring, life-changing experience for every explorer.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s most trusted and innovative travel company, blending adventure with sustainability.
