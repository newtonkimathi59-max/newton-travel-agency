Our Global Destinations

From the wild plains of Africa and tranquil islands of the Indian Ocean, to vibrant cities in Europe and Asia’s cultural hubs — at Newton Travel Agency, we’ve carefully selected 20 of the world’s most inspiring destinations. Let your next great adventure begin here.

Maasai Mara, Kenya

Roam the golden savannahs of the Maasai Mara, witness the Great Migration in action and meet Maasai warriors who guide you through authentic wildlife encounters.

Cape Town, South Africa

Where ocean meets mountain — from Table Mountain hikes and colourful Bo-Kaap streets, to wine estates and penguins at Boulders Beach.

Paris, France

The City of Light — stroll along the Seine, marvel at the Eiffel Tower and dive into world-class cafés, museums and boutiques.

Marrakech, Morocco

A sensory journey of spice and colour — wander through the Medina’s souks, relax in a riad and discover the Sahara on a desert excursion.

London, UK

From royal palaces to vibrant street art — London melds historic grandeur with modern flair and world-class cuisine.

Tokyo, Japan

A neon wonderland of tradition and innovation — explore ancient temples by day and modern skyscrapers by night.

Dubai, UAE

Luxury and desert adventure wrapped into one — world-class architecture, shopping, dune safaris and star-studded sights.

New York City, USA

The city that never sleeps — Broadway shows, skyline views, iconic landmarks and multicultural neighbourhoods await.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Fun, sun and carnival spirit — soak in panoramic views from Christ the Redeemer and dance along the famous beaches of Copacabana and Ipanema.

Sydney, Australia

Opera House, harbour cruises, Bondi Beach and coastal walks — this sun-lit city combines relaxed Aussie style with global energy.

Bali, Indonesia

Lush rice terraces, sacred temples and beachy rituals — Bali is island life at its most vibrant and soulful.

Maldives

Crystal-clear lagoons, over-water villas and palm-lined atolls — your haven of peace and luxury in the Indian Ocean.

Santorini, Greece

White-washed houses, cobalt domes and caldera views — a romantic cliff-side escape like no other.

Istanbul, Turkey

A city straddling continents — historic mosques, vibrant bazaars, Bosphorus ferries and deep-rooted culture in one place.

Cairo, Egypt

Walk among ancient wonders — the Pyramids, the Sphinx and the Nile’s timeless embrace await your discovery.

Kyoto, Japan

Zen gardens, historic temples and traditional tea houses — Kyoto offers a tranquil counterpoint to modern Tokyo.

Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe

“The Smoke That Thunders” — one of the world’s largest waterfalls, surrounded by lush rainforest and thrilling adventure.

Machu Picchu, Peru

High in the Andes, this ancient Incan city shrouded in mist invites discovery, history and breathtaking views.

Grand Canyon, USA

A natural masterpiece carved by time — hues of rock and expansive vistas create an unforgettable journey into the earth’s story.

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

Dive into the world’s largest coral reef system — vibrant marine life, coral gardens and underwater adventure await.
